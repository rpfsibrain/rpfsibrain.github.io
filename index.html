I design and model nature‑inspired magnetic soft robotic swimmers and swarms, and develop computational methods for fluid–structure interaction and hard‑magnetic soft materials. I supervise students, give workshops, and collaborate across mechanics, materials and robotics.

Research Interests

Magnetic soft robotic swimmers (nature‑inspired)
Collective behavior & swarms
Computational FSI at low Reynolds number
Hard‑magnetic soft materials (constitutive models)
Damage & viscoplastic modeling of soft materials
Data‑driven/ML for fluids & materials
